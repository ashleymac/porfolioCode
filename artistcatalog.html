FROM SKETCH TO SCREEN

This 40-page artist anthology details the work of Colleen Atwood, a costume designer who is a staple in Hollywood blockbusters and has been nominated for 11 Oscars. This book showcases her three victories: Chicago, Memoirs of a Geisha, and Tim Burton's Alice in Wonderland. I was inspired by high-fashion editorial spreads that reflected the modern gothic style of her costumes.
