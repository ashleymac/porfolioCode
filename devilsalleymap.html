THE DEVIL'S ALLEY WAYFINDING MAP

This map tracks the disappearances associated with the mysterious Bermuda Triangle. The coding system is divided into three categories: planes, ships, and submarines and each story is color-coded according to the vessel that was lost. The triangular shape can be seen throughout the entire piece including the outline of the landmasses and the map key.
