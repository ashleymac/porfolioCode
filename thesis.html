UNTOLD STORIES

I designed a fully responsive website chronicling a series of untold stories from the day of the September 11th attacks. The project was a self-guided 10-week process including extensive research, concept development and execution. My objective was to portray this historic event in a modern way, while still respecting the sensitive subject matter. I kept a minimal color palette to allow the photography to help the stories. I treated each story with a different concept to provide a unique experience on every page.
